Our Story

Preserving Punjabi craftsmanship since 1995

Founded in the heart of Punjab, Bhumika Jewellers has been creating exquisite traditional jewelry for over 25 years. Our master craftsmen combine age-old techniques with contemporary designs to bring you pieces that tell a story.

Each jewelry piece is crafted with the same dedication and love that goes into preparing a Punjabi feast - with attention to every detail and a celebration of abundance.
